Cat-ch3

A match-3 game full of kitties!

  1. Click 'PLAY' to start the game.
  2. Match at least 3 kitties by swapping them and destroy the pink background behind them.
  3. Simply click the icons you want to swap.
  4. You will get 10 points for each icon in the match. If an icon belongs to two matches, the points will be counted double.
  5. If you do not manage to destroy the entire pink background in 20 moves (or less) or if there are no more valid moves to make, you lose!
  6. The background will be destroyed only behind the matches directly created by you, not the ones that might follow. Similarly, you will only get points for this kind of matches.
  7. You can restart the game clicking the "New game" button
  8. When you finish the game, a kitty will make its appearence, but you will have to show a bit of patience.
