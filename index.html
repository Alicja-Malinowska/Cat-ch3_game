Cat-ch3

A match-3 game full of kitties!

How to play the game
  1. Click 'PLAY' to start the game.
  2. The goal of the game is to destroy the pink background on the game board by matching cat icons on top of it. You have 20 moves to do that.
  3. You need to match at least 3 same cat icons by swapping two icons on the board.
  4. To swap, click an icon, and next click an icon adjacent to it (top, bottom, left or right). If the swapped icons create a match, the matched icons will disappear (together with the pink background under them) and the board will be refilled with new icons.
  5. You will get 10 points for each icon in the match. If an icon belongs to two matches, the points will be counted double.
  6. If you do not manage to destroy the entrire pink background in 20 moves or if there are no more valid moves to make, you lose!
  7. You can restart the game clicking the "New game" button
  8. When you finish the game, a kitty will make its appearence, but you will have to show a bit of patience.
